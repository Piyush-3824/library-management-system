ℹ About the Library

📖

About Us

Established in 1985, the University Central Library holds over 50,000 books, journals and academic publications across all disciplines.

📗

Book Borrowing

Borrow up to 5 books for 14 days. Renew online for up to 2 additional periods if no holds exist.

🖥

Digital Resources

Access thousands of e-books and research databases online 24/7 using your university email credentials.

🪑

Reading Rooms

Quiet, air-conditioned reading rooms with 300+ seats for focused study and research. Available Mon–Sat.

📋

Library Rules

Return books on time. Maintain silence. Lost books incur replacement cost + ₹200 penalty. Library card required.

🔬

Research Support

Our librarians provide personalized research guidance, citation management, and thesis support sessions.
